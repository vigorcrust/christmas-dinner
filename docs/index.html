Cucumbers with Pickled Ginger and Crab
  • Cucumbers
  • Mayonnaise
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Shallot minced
  • Chives minced
  • Thai chili paste
  • Crabmeat cooked
  • Ginger pickled
  • Parsley

Slice cucumber. Mix mayo, oil, lemon juice, shallots, chili paste, chives, salt. Add crab meat und mix - don't break up the big lumbs. Place on cucumber and top with ginger and parsley. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Cauliflower Soup
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cauliflower
  • Chicken stock
  • Cream/Milk
  • Thyme, Bay leaf

Fry onions until soft, add garlic until fragrant. Add everything except cream and cook until tender ~20min. Remove thyme and bay leaf and blend with immersion blender until smooth and add cream. Finish of with fried thyme in olive oil.
